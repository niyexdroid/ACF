Welcome to Welfare Established Since 2022

The creation of the Abosedeaina Charity Foundation was as a result of a tragic incident that befell my family 18 years ago. The inspiration for the foundation's name was to commemorate my late sister (Aina Abosede). She was the quietest, most well-mannered and cheerful among my siblings. She was happily married but unfortunately developed eclampsia during her pregnancy period. It became even more complicated during labour; She lost her sight and became unconscious. She had an emergency Caesarean Section but unfortunately, we lost both her and the baby.

It was heartbreaking news for the family especially my mother, because some few years back, she also lost her first daughter, my elder sister who died a few days after giving birth to her fourth child. Ever since then, I have always had it in mind to start a foundation in their memory.
